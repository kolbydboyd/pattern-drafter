Pattern Tester Program

Get free patterns. Sew them. Tell us how they fit. Get featured.

How It Works

01

Apply

Tell us about your sewing experience and what machines you have. Takes 2 minutes.

02

Get Approved

We review applications weekly. Approved testers get a free pattern credit and a garment assignment.

03

Sew a Muslin

Generate your pattern, print it, and sew a test garment. Cheap fabric, no finishing needed.

04

Submit Feedback

Rate the fit, flag problem areas, and upload photos. Your feedback directly improves the pattern.

05

Get Featured

Opt in and your photos appear in our gallery + social channels. Tag us and we share your makes.

What Testers Get

✉ Free pattern credit for every assignment completed
★ Featured on the site in the tester gallery
📷 Social media features on our Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest
💬 Direct input into pattern improvements
👤 Early access to new garments before public release
