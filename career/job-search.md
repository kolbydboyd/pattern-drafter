## Target Companies

### AI Coding Tools — DevRel / Developer Advocate
These companies want people who have a real story using their tools or competing tools.

- **Anthropic** — Claude Code team specifically. You are the user story.
- **Cursor** — Similar profile. AI-assisted coding tools.
- **GitHub** — Copilot team. Developer experience and advocacy.
- **Replit** — Heavily focused on non-traditional developers building real things.
- **Bolt / StackBlitz** — AI builder product, same audience.
- **Lovable** — Built for product people who ship with AI. You are their customer and advocate.
- **v0 / Vercel** — If familiar with their tooling. Solutions or DevRel.

### Infrastructure They Use Deeply — Solutions / TAM
You have used these in production. That is more credible than a certification.

- **Cloudflare** — Solutions Engineering. You ran Pages Functions in production.
- **Supabase** — Solutions or Customer Success. You have 18 migrations in prod.
- **Stripe** — Solutions Engineer. End-to-end Stripe integration live and charging.
- **PostHog** — Solutions or Customer Success. 23 custom events, A/B tests, session recording live.
- **Resend** — Small team, growing fast. Email automation in production.
- **AWS** — Lambda specifically. Solutions Architect Associate cert would strengthen this path.

### Fashion / Apparel Tech — Domain Expertise Is Rare in Engineering
Pattern drafting + garment construction knowledge is genuinely uncommon in tech candidates.

- **True Fit** — AI fit recommendation. Your garment engine + sizing knowledge is directly relevant.
- **Clo3D / Browzwear** — 3D garment simulation software. Your pattern geometry knowledge is a bridge.
- **Unspun** — Custom-fit jeans startup. You built a custom-fit jeans module.
- **Stitch Fix** — Technical roles where domain expertise matters.
- **ThredUp** — Resale / recommerce. Technical product roles.
- **Simplicity / McCall's / Butterick** — Legacy pattern companies attempting digital transformation. You built what they have not.

### Startups — Technical PM / Solutions
Any Seed to Series B SaaS that uses the same stack (Supabase + Stripe + Cloudflare) would hire you to help customers implement, onboard, or scale their use.

- Search: "Supabase + Stripe startup" on LinkedIn, AngelList, or Wellfound
- E-commerce infrastructure: Shopify ecosystem, Gorgias, Klaviyo
- EdTech / maker communities: Skillshare, Teachable, Maven
- Vertical SaaS in any industry you understand (aviation, facilities, logistics)

## How to Apply

### For Developer Advocate roles
- Write a detailed post on LinkedIn or X about building a production SaaS with Claude Code. Be specific. Show the infra diagram. Explain what AI-assisted development actually means. Tag Anthropic, Cursor, Replit.
- Record a Loom walkthrough (5-10 min) of the product: show the pattern engine, show a checkout, show the PDF output. Put this as your LinkedIn Featured section.
- Cold email the DevRel or Developer Experience team directly. Not HR.

### For Technical PM / Solutions Engineer
- Apply via LinkedIn and company careers pages.
- In the cover note: one paragraph. "I built [product]. Here is the URL. Here are the specific tools I used. I am not a traditional developer. I am looking for a role where shipping fast and understanding the full stack matters more than LeetCode."
- Prepare to demo the product live in interviews.

### For AI-Native / Contract Builder roles
- Post on X and LinkedIn: "I use Claude Code to ship production SaaS products. Available for contract." Include the URL and one screenshot.
- Search "AI engineer" or "AI product builder" on Contra, Toptal, Braintrust, and Upwork.
- Target founder-led companies (5-30 people) building internal tools or early-stage products.
