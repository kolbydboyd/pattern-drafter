Pattern Tester Agreement

Version 1.0. Effective 2026-05-29.

1. Parties

This agreement is between People's Patterns LLC ("People's Patterns," "we," "us") and the individual ("Pattern Tester," "you") who has applied to and been accepted into the Pattern Tester Program.

2. What We Offer

Accepted testers receive full access to the entire People's Patterns pattern catalog — including unreleased and untested patterns — for as long as they are active participants. On top of that, we pay $30 for every pattern you complete and submit with the required deliverables.

3. Nature of Engagement

You are engaged as an independent contractor, not an employee. You set your own schedule, work at your own location, and use your own equipment and materials. People's Patterns does not withhold income taxes, Social Security, Medicare, or any other taxes on your behalf. You are solely responsible for all tax obligations arising from compensation received under this agreement.

4. Catalog Access

Upon approval, you receive full access to all People's Patterns digital pattern files, including patterns not yet publicly released, for the duration of your active participation. This is a standing benefit, not tied to any individual test. Access is contingent on good-faith participation and may be revoked if you become inactive, violate the confidentiality terms, or are removed from the program.

5. Compensation

People's Patterns will pay you $30 USD per completed, submitted pattern test. Compensation is per pattern assigned. If you complete multiple tests, each is paid separately.

Payment is issued upon delivery of all three required deliverables for a given test (see Section 6). If the program's monthly paid-test cap has been reached, your completed test is still accepted, credited to your tester record, and paid in the following billing cycle.

Payment method will be communicated at the time of assignment approval.

6. Deliverables

For each assigned pattern test, you agree to submit all of the following:

(a) A test garment. A completed muslin or finished garment sewn using the assigned pattern and your recorded body measurements. For muslins, full finishing is not required, but all fit-relevant seams must be sewn. The garment must be wearable enough to assess fit accurately.

(b) Written fit feedback. A structured written assessment comparing the sewn garment to your entered measurements. This must note where the garment fits as expected, where it does not, and any drafting issues observed (ease, seam placement, geometry problems, instruction gaps). Brief single-line responses are not sufficient.

(c) Your measurements. The exact body measurements used to generate the pattern, as entered in the People's Patterns system.

(d) Photos or video. At least one clear, usable photo or short video of the finished garment worn on your body. Multiple angles are preferred. We may request additional photos before releasing payment.

7. Timeline

You agree to complete and submit each assigned test within ____ days of receiving the pattern file. The specific window for each assignment is confirmed at assignment time. If you cannot complete the test by the deadline, contact us before it passes so we can extend or reassign.

8. Photo and Media Rights

By submitting photos or video as part of a pattern test, you grant People's Patterns LLC a perpetual, royalty-free, worldwide, non-exclusive license to use, reproduce, display, distribute, and create derivative works from your submitted media, including images and video in which your likeness appears.

This license covers use on the People's Patterns website, Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, paid advertising (Meta Ads, TikTok Ads, Pinterest Ads, and similar platforms), email marketing, and any other promotional context People's Patterns uses now or in the future.

You represent that you own or have the right to grant this license for all submitted media, and that the media does not infringe any third-party rights.

You retain ownership of your original photos and videos. This license does not authorize People's Patterns to sell your media to third parties as standalone content.

Consent to these terms is required to participate in the program. You confirm acceptance by checking the media consent box on the application form.

9. Confidentiality

All pattern files you access through this program, whether tested or not, are confidential. You agree not to copy, share, redistribute, sell, or otherwise make any People's Patterns pattern file available to any third party, in any form, for any reason. This obligation survives the end of your participation in the program.

You may share photos and written feedback about finished garments you have sewn, subject to the media rights terms in Section 8.

10. Termination

Either party may end this arrangement at any time by written notice (email is sufficient). People's Patterns may revoke catalog access immediately if you violate the confidentiality terms or act in bad faith. Compensation for completed and accepted tests submitted before termination will still be paid.

11. No Warranty

Patterns are provided for testing purposes. People's Patterns is not liable for materials costs, time, or other expenses you incur if a test garment does not turn out as expected. Pattern testing inherently means working with patterns that may contain errors. You accept that risk as part of participating in the program.

12. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the United States. Any disputes arising from this agreement will be resolved through good-faith negotiation first. If negotiation fails, disputes will be submitted to binding arbitration.
