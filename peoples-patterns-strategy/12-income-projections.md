## Brother Partnership

A second person (brother/friend/collaborator) accelerates every bottleneck.

### Muslin Testing

| Metric | Without Brother | With Brother | Impact |
|--------|----------------|--------------|--------|
| Muslins per week (you alone) | 1 | 2 | 2× throughput |
| Body measurement sets tested | 1 | 2 | Different bodies = better validation |
| Weeks to validate 6 launch patterns | 6 | 3 | Launch 3 weeks earlier |
| Weeks to validate all 23 modules | 23 | 12 | Half the time to full catalog |

### Fit Feedback Value

| Metric | Without Brother | With Brother | Impact |
|--------|----------------|--------------|--------|
| Unique body types in fit data | 1 | 2 | Critical for diverse sizing |
| Fit reports before launch | 6 | 12 | 2× credibility data |
| Edge cases caught (different proportions) | Some | More | His body finds bugs yours doesn't |

### Content Production

| Metric | Without Brother | With Brother | Impact |
|--------|----------------|--------------|--------|
| Sew-along filming (camera operator) | Tripod only | 2nd angle + hands | Way better video quality |
| Flat lay photography | You style + shoot | One sews, one shoots | Parallel production |
| Pattern test reviews | Self-review only | Independent tester | Unbiased feedback for marketing |

### Instruction Quality

| Metric | Without Brother | With Brother | Impact |
|--------|----------------|--------------|--------|
| Beginner perspective | No (you know sewing) | Yes (learning from scratch) | His confusion = your UX bugs |
| Instructions written for beginners? | Hard to tell | Verified by one | Massive value for target audience |

### Suggested Arrangement

| Phase | Plan | Rationale |
|-------|------|-----------|
| Phase 1: Learning (Month 1–2) | You teach, he learns | Investment in future throughput |
| Phase 2: Testing (Month 3+) | He tests independently | You focus on dev + content |
| Compensation model | Revenue share or per-muslin fee | e.g. $25/muslin or 10% of pattern rev |
| Long term | Co-creator, pattern tester, content | Could become a real team |
