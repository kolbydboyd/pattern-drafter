## Priority Order for Publishing

**Phase 1 (launch week):** Articles 1, 45, 53, 31, 3, 4
These cover the core questions a new visitor will have: how to measure, how our tool works, how to print, how much fabric, what is ease, what is seam allowance.

**Phase 2 (first month):** Articles 32-44 (one per garment)
Each garment guide drives traffic to that specific pattern page.

**Phase 3 (ongoing):** Technique tutorials (11-24), fabric guides (25-30), fit articles (46-52)
Steady content that builds authority and backlinks.

**Phase 4 (growth):** Capsule wardrobe and planning articles (56-58)
Drives bundle and subscription conversions.
